What is WCB?
WCB means Wall Climb Boost.
It is one of the Roblox Parkour skills.
To perform, You must press the spacebar twice as fast as possible
(And turn your camera at the same time).
It gives you a lot of vertical velocity boost.



What is WCB MS?
It is the time between pressing the space bar and pressing it again.
As the name suggests, the unit is milliseconds(MS, 0.001 sec).
A lower ms means faster presses.
